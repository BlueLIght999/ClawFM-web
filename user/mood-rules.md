# Mood → Music Rules

## Chill / Relax
Genre: ambient, acoustic, lo-fi
Tempo: slow (60-90 BPM)
Examples: soft piano, nature sounds, mellow electronic

## Energy / Upbeat
Genre: pop, rock, electronic
Tempo: medium-fast (120-140 BPM)
Examples: driving beats, bright melodies

## Focus / Study
Genre: instrumental, classical, ambient
Tempo: steady (80-110 BPM)
Examples: piano works, post-rock, minimal electronic

## Melancholy / Rainy Day
Genre: indie, folk, slowcore
Tempo: slow (60-80 BPM)
Examples: sad guitar, rainy jazz, atmospheric

## Party / Celebration
Genre: dance, hip-hop, funk
Tempo: fast (120-160 BPM)
Examples: club bangers, classic disco

## Late Night / Insomnia
Genre: jazz, ambient, dream pop
Tempo: very slow (50-70 BPM)
Examples: Miles Davis, Brian Eno, Beach House
